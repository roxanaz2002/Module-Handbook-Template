\subsection{Introduction to Potions}

\courseinfo{Introduction to Potions}{POT101}{3}{48}

\objects{
This course introduces the fundamental principles of potion-making, including ingredient preparation, brewing techniques, and safety precautions. 
Students will explore both traditional and modern applications of potions, from healing remedies to transformation elixirs. 
Emphasis will be placed on understanding the chemical interactions between magical components and achieving precise results through careful brewing methods.}

\courseschedule{
1  & Basics of Potion-Making & Safety protocols, tools, and equipment & 2 \\ \hline
2  & Ingredients & Identification and properties of common ingredients & 4 \\ \hline
3  & Brewing Techniques & Stirring, heating, and timing techniques & 2 \\ \hline
4  & Healing Potions & Recipes and uses of restorative potions & 4 \\ \hline
5  & Love Potions & Theory and ethics behind amorous elixirs & 2 \\ \hline
6  & Antidotes & Crafting and administering antidotes & 4 \\ \hline
7  & Polyjuice Potion & Recipe, preparation, and limitations & 2 \\ \hline
8  & Invisibility Potions & Brewing techniques for concealment & 4 \\ \hline
9  & Memory Modifiers & Potions that alter memory & 2 \\ \hline
10 & Aging Potions & Effects of time-manipulating potions & 4 \\ \hline
11 & Strength and Energy Elixirs & Physical enhancement through magic & 2 \\ \hline
12 & Dangerous Potions & Handling volatile or toxic mixtures & 4 \\ \hline
13 & Potion Testing & Evaluating potency and side effects & 2 \\ \hline
14 & Potions in Magical History & Historical significance of various brews & 4 \\ \hline
15 & Advanced Potion-Making & Introduction to experimental brewing & 2 \\ \hline
16 & Final Practical Assessment & Brewing and testing a custom potion & 4 
}

\subsection{Advanced Spellcasting}

\courseinfo{Advanced Spellcasting}{CHM301}{4}{64}

\objects{
The course “Advanced Spellcasting” aims to deepen students’ mastery of complex incantations and charm work. 
Students will learn advanced techniques to enhance spell accuracy, strength, and control. The curriculum emphasizes the theory behind spell construction, wordless casting, and wandless magic, while exploring the historical evolution of modern spells. 
Students are encouraged to develop resilience when facing challenging magic and to cultivate responsibility in using powerful spells. This course also explores spell modifications, counter-charms, and protective enchantments for real-world applications.
}

\courseschedule{
1  & Theory of Spell Construction & Understanding spell components and syntax & 2 \\ \hline
2  & Advanced Wand Movements & Precision and speed in spellcasting & 4 \\ \hline
3  & Non-verbal Incantations & Fundamentals of wordless magic & 4 \\ \hline
4  & Wandless Magic & Techniques for casting without a wand & 2 \\ \hline
5  & Shield Charms & Casting and maintaining protective barriers & 4 \\ \hline
6  & Counter-Curses & Neutralizing harmful spells & 4 \\ \hline
7  & Enchantments & Advanced charm work for objects & 2 \\ \hline
8  & Disillusionment Charm & Techniques for personal invisibility & 4 \\ \hline
9  & Patronus Charm & Summoning protective guardians & 2 \\ \hline
10 & Spell Modifications & Tweaking and combining existing spells & 4 \\ \hline
11 & Time-Freezing Charms & Delicate manipulation of temporal magic & 2 \\ \hline
12 & Fiendfyre Control & Containing and extinguishing dark fire & 4 \\ \hline
13 & Spell Theory in Duels & Strategy and spell combinations for defense & 2 \\ \hline
14 & Ethical Spellcasting & Discussions on responsible magic use & 4 \\ \hline
15 & Experimental Spell Creation & Designing custom incantations & 4 \\ \hline
16 & Final Practical Exam & Demonstration of learned spells & 4 
}
